About me

Hello, I'm Sam. Growing up, I was always around computers but never considered to work with them for a living. At first, my childhood dream was to be a commercial pilot but one day my friend and I found a PC game that we ended up liking and stuck to it. As time went on, I became more experienced with computers slowly discovered how it can help people and the environment in many ways. It may be a far-fetched fantasy, but I hope one day I'll be able to advance technology further in a way to allow computers to visualize and display human thoughts so we can better understand our minds and our selves.
