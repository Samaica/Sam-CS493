How can I cook spaghetti?

  1. Boil water in a large pot.
    • Use a least 4 quartz of water for every pounds of noodles.
    • Put on high heat.
  2. Salt the water with at least a tablespoon-more is fine.
  3. Add the pasta.
  4. Stir the pasta.
  5. Drain the pasta.
